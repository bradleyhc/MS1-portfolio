More about Bradley

After 6 successful years within a fast paced and intense Events industry, Covid-19 has somewhat disrupted the industry and caused agencies, clients and suppliers to rethink their offering.

With my existing knowledge and experience in the technical aspects of the role, I have found myself continually moving towards a more 'digital first' approach to work and day to day life. Not only does my role as Producer now see me researching new virtual platforms and finding innovative ways to deliver client programs, but I am also tapping into my inner need to 'create', by picking up previous entreprenuerial website ventures and dusting off past coding knowledge.

In doing so, I have recently re-discovered my love for code and the endless possibilities it provides us in creating beautifully designed websites that users cannot take their eyes / fingers off! It wasn't long then, with this mindset and a disrupted and somewhat shaken industry, that the realisation of a different life course hit me ... so I enrolled in a code bootcamp, and here I am.

Alongside the inner-geek I have since coaxed back into my everyday life, I also enjoy dreaming up big ideas for future projects and businesses, and (enjoyed) travelling the world to discover what I'm missing out on in life.
